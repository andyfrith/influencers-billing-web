# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

A rugged, outdoorsy aesthetic for "Hiking Enthusiasts." The design should feel grounded and tactile, mimicking the feel of a field guide or trail map.

## Color Palette

The colors are inspired by natural landscapes.

- Use **Primary** for navigation and primary action buttons.
- Use **Secondary** for borders and subtle accents.
- Use **Accent** for alerts, notifications, or "Book Now" highlights.

## Typography

- **Headings:** High-contrast serifs to give a sense of heritage and adventure.
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif for high legibility during mobile use on the trail.

## Imagery & UI Patterns

- Use "Soft Mist" background to prevent eye strain.
- Components should have a subtle 8px radius to feel friendly but structured.
- Card components should use high-quality, full-bleed nature photography.
