# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Ideal for hiking enthusiasts, travel guides, and nature-focused content. The design mimics the tactile feel of a high-end field guide or trail map.

## UI Patterns

- **Glassmorphism Cards:** Use semi-transparent card backgrounds with a backdrop-blur effect to sit over high-resolution nature photography.
- **Organic Shadows:** Shadows should be soft and wide to suggest natural depth.
- **Full-Bleed Imagery:** Use large, immersive background images that span the full width of the viewport.
