# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Industrial and creative, designed for photography studios, ad agencies, or co-working spaces. It feels like a functional physical workspace.

## UI Patterns

- **Exposed Structure:** Visible grid lines and labels like "01", "02" for section ordering.
- **High-Impact Type:** Large, bold headings that take up 50% of the hero section width.
- **Workspace Imagery:** Focus on showing the "process"—sketches, gear, and raw materials.
