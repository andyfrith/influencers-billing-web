# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Inspired by Brutalist architecture, this design is raw, honest, and intentionally "unpolished." It is perfect for underground music, indie labels, or avant-garde architecture portfolios.

## UI Patterns

- **No Shadows:** Elements are flat and grounded; no depth or gradients are allowed.
- **Heavy Borders:** All containers use a thick 2px-4px black border.
- **Hover States:** Intense color inversions on hover rather than subtle transitions.
