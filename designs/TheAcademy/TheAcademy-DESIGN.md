# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Professional and authoritative for universities, research papers, or online courses. It mimics the look of a prestigious physical institution.

## UI Patterns

- **Emblem Usage:** Navigation should feature a central, prominent logo or "seal" placement.
- **Multi-Level Menus:** Clear, hierarchical navigation that allows for deep discovery of departments or topics.
- **Serif/Sans Balance:** High-contrast serif for section titles and very clean sans-serif for course descriptions.
