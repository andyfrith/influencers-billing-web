# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Heavy, masculine, and industrial. Best for construction firms, custom metalwork, or gym equipment.

## UI Patterns

- **Metallic Gradients:** Use subtle linear gradients to mimic brushed steel or aluminum.
- **Rivet Accents:** Small dot icons in the corners of containers to simulate bolts or rivets.
- **Hard Grids:** No border-radius; everything is built on 90-degree angles.
