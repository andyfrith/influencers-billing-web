# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Perfect for single-product launches or bold manifestos. Everything revolves around one massive central element.

## UI Patterns

- **Center-Focus:** One primary headline and one primary CTA dominate the entire initial viewport.
- **Oversized Type:** Headings should break across lines to create a visual "block."
- **Scroll-Triggered Reveals:** Content sections "slide up" from behind the primary monolith.
