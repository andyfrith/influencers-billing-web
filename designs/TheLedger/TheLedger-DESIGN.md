# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Focuses on long-form reading and traditional typesetting. Perfect for personal blogs, newsletters, or professional advice where trust is built through text.

## UI Patterns

- **Vertical Rhythm:** Strict adherence to line-heights ensures a comfortable reading flow.
- **Single Column:** Content is centered with generous margins to focus the user's attention.
- **Typography Focus:** Minimal use of decorative icons; rely on font weights and styles to create hierarchy.
