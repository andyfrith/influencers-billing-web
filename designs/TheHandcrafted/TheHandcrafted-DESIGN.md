# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Perfect for Etsy sellers, woodworking, or artisanal bakeries. It feels human, warm, and approachable.

## UI Patterns

- **Stitched Borders:** Use dashed lines for borders to mimic sewing.
- **Hand-Drawn Icons:** Use icons that look like sketches or pen drawings.
- **Warm Overlays:** Apply a warm filter to all product photography.
