# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Designed for collectors and massive digital libraries. It focuses on the sheer volume of items while maintaining a structured grid.

## UI Patterns

- **Minimal Gutter Spacing:** Keep cards close together to emphasize the "collection."
- **Hover Reveal:** Detailed information should only appear when the user hovers over an item.
- **Robust Filtering:** A persistent sidebar for sorting large datasets.
