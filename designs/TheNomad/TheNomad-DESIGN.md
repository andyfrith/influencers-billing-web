# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

A structural foundation for overland travel, camping gear, or rugged outdoor lifestyle brands. It feels tactile, weathered, and reliable.

## UI Patterns

- **Canvas Textures:** Use subtle burlap or canvas textures on section backgrounds.
- **Worn Edges:** Apply very slight, irregular border-radii (e.g., `4px 2px 5px 3px`) to buttons to simulate hand-crafted gear.
- **Stamp-Style Icons:** Iconography should look like ink stamps or branded leather marks.
