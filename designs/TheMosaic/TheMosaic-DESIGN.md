# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

A grid-focused layout for personal journals, food blogs, or hobbyist collections. It feels organized yet varied.

## UI Patterns

- **Tiled Backgrounds:** Use repeating subtle patterns behind content sections.
- **Card-Style Feed:** Every entry is a self-contained card with an image header.
- **Colorful Tags:** High-contrast tags to categorize content at a glance.
