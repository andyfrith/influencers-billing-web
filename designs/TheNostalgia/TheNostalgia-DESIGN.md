# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Mimics early 90s operating systems and paper catalogs. Best for vintage shops or tech history sites.

## UI Patterns

- **Inset Borders:** Containers use "bevel and emboss" style borders to appear 3D.
- **Paper Textures:** Backgrounds should include a faint grain or noise texture.
- **Fixed Windows:** Content boxes appear as draggable or stackable "windows" rather than fluid sections.
