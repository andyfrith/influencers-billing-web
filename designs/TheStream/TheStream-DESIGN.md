# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Ideal for recipe aggregators or daily inspiration boards. It is optimized for "infinite scroll" behavior to keep users engaged with a constant flow.

## UI Patterns

- **Masonry Flow:** Cards vary in height based on content to eliminate wasted vertical space.
- **Compact Gutters:** Spacing between cards remains small (8px-12px) to maximize content visibility.
- **Quick-Save Actions:** Visible "save" or "like" icons appear on hover for immediate interaction.
