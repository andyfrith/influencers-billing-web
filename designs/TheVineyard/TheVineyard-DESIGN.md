# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Classic and aged. Ideal for wineries, heritage brands, or artisanal craftsmanship. It evokes a sense of time and quality.

## UI Patterns

- **Textured Paper:** Use a subtle "linen" or "parchment" texture for section backgrounds.
- **Golden Ratio Layout:** Strictly align product photography with the golden ratio.
- **Ornate Dividers:** Use stylized horizontal rules (e.g., a simple leaf or dot motif) between sections.
