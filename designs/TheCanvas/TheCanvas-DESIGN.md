# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

The "invisible UI" for artists and architects. The structure is dictated by the artwork itself.

## UI Patterns

- **Extreme Padding:** Sections should be separated by massive gaps.
- **Borderless UI:** No visible containers; let the imagery define the space.
- **Minimalist Navigation:** A simple text-based menu that stays out of the way of the content.
