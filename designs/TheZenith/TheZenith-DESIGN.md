# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Uses the Golden Ratio to achieve mathematical harmony. It is the gold standard for B2B SaaS and high-level corporate portfolios.

## UI Patterns

- **Golden Ratio Scaling:** All margins and containers follow the 1.618 ratio.
- **Ghost Buttons:** Secondary actions use transparent backgrounds with 1px borders.
- **Thin-Stroke Icons:** Ultra-light iconography to maintain a clean, high-tech look.
