# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Ideal for herbal medicine, skincare, or organic chemistry blogs. It balances the beauty of nature with the rigor of science.

## UI Patterns

- **Etched Illustrations:** Use thin-line botanical sketches as decorative background elements.
- **Glass Containers:** Use high-transparency surface colors with thin white borders to mimic lab glassware.
- **Serif Labels:** Use small-caps serif typography for all functional labels (e.g., "CATEGORIES," "LOG IN").
