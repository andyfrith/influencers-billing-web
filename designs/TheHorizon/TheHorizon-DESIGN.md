# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Designed for cinematic storytelling, luxury travel, or film projects. This template prioritizes visual flow and high-motion media.

## UI Patterns

- **Cinematic Snap:** Use vertical or horizontal "scroll snapping" to ensure every section feels like a movie frame.
- **Negative Space:** Elements are pushed to the edges (safe zones) to keep the central visuals clear.
- **Minimal Text:** Focus on short, punchy headings with high line-height body text.
