# DESIGN RATIONALE

## Core Concept

Designed for high-end art dealers and museum exhibits. It treats the browser window like a physical gallery wall where items are curated rather than just listed.

## UI Patterns

- **Variable Grid Sizing:** Avoid uniform rows. Use a "broken grid" where images vary between 33%, 50%, and 66% width.
- **Vertical Labels:** Use vertical text orientation for image captions to mimic gallery placards.
- **Large Page Numbers:** Use oversized serif numbers for pagination to emphasize the sequence of a collection.
